Welcome to the Novadev Blog!

I'm Morgan, a Full Stack Software Engineer (github.com/madaley1)!

I've attempted blogging before, but it was always more complex than I wanted, and I felt like due to the complexity I needed to do more than just throw my thoughts out there, and I knew I wasn't wanting to do a fully professional devblog where you SEO optimize and tag everything perfectly for searchability, I just wanted a place to put some thoughts every once and a while.

I found some other platforms, but none really were both simple and self-hosted, all required config and themes, or were restricted to a specific platform/site, so I decided I'd just make an unfancy, non-complex platform where you just write markdown and push.

You can of course customize it via CSS, and you can edit the HTML outside of the autogenerated post areas, but otherwise it's meant to just essentially be a place to put text.

Feel free to look around, or check out the repo for how to set up your own nova blog!

Also the only reason my domain isn't novadev.github.io is because it was already taken, idk if it's even used but hopefully it'll come back open someday so I can steal it >:)
